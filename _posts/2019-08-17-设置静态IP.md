> ## linux 设置静态 IP

> 打开虚拟机设置

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/doudio/note/master/Linux/centos/img/staticIP.png)

> 开启 linux 图形界面

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/doudio/note/master/Linux/centos/img/linuxView.png)

> 设置好后将网卡重启

```shell
/etc/init.d/network restart
```
